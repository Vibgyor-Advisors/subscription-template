About Program

The objective of this program is to create a Global Mobilisation of youth around the world to take actions for good cause. Through our programs we aim to make Global Opportunities accessible to everyone and create long lasting partnerships across communities for a better future. Since the launch of Sustainable Development Goals by the United Nations in 2015 hundreds of new opportunities have been generated for youth around the world. RYF Country Ambassador Program is for young dynamic leaders who can come forward to join the mission of Rising Youth Foundation. We are looking for young leaders across the globe who will be an agent of change to promote several ongoing programs in their respective country, adhering to the agenda of engaging maximum number of youth in the social sector. One year long program provides the opportunity to amplify your actions and be a part of a dynamically growing organisation. Country ambassadors will be benefitted and supported by the RYF community consistently to take actions in their local community using our resources. 

Roles & Responsibilities

This program focuses on amplifying our mission in different countries. Primarily , the ambassadors will represent RYF at their respective countries and help us raise awareness , promote and implement our projects throughout their nation.

01.

Help us understand the culture and market of the  respective country to implement our programs. 

02.

Promote and spread the cause of RYF in your country, create awareness, identify changemakers, promote inspiring stories.

03.

Plan and organize different campaigns and programs to create awareness about global issues and  ensure access to opportunities in your locality.

04.

Identify and forge potential partnerships with different student bodies, youth groups, and social organizations, government departments, to undertake mutually beneficial programs.

05.

Share and circulate relevant, local and global opportunities to your community and encourage them to participate in several programs.

06.

Manage the RYF social media platforms and groups for your country.

Incentives & Benefits

It’s not just about the duties and responsibilities, what matters most is your learning which happens during the program. This is opportunity for you if you believe you are the one who believe in
  • Accrue experience working at an international social landscape, while leading projects with people of diverse backgrounds and cultures.
  • Excellent opportunity to build high level connections and Enhance your networks and learn from global leaders and resource persons.
  • Attend and represent your country and RYF to various national and international programs.
  • Receive certificates from RYF upon successful completion of the term and nominations to participate in different programs.
  • Get the opportunity to showcase your talent, achievements and experience with millions of youth through RYF and boost your social profile.
  • Represent your country at the international level and get a kick-start for your international career, receive support to launch your own projects.
  • Opportunity to attend and host hundreds of webinars for students across the globe.

01.

Country Ambassadors are responsible for fostering relationships with the youth and social organisations  in their respective countries.

02.

A Country Ambassador acts as a representative  and therefore should possess certain skill sets.

03.

Enthusiastic to spread the cause of RYF; which is creating awareness about SDGs, equal opportunities for everyone, amplifying the voice of changemakers.

05.
